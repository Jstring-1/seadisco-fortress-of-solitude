How SeaDisco Works

Basic Search

Type anything into the main search box — artist name, album title, label, genre, or a mix. SeaDisco searches the entire Discogs database of over 15 million releases.

Advanced Search

Click Advanced to unlock targeted fields:

  • Artist — search specifically by artist name
  • Release Title — search by album or single title
  • Label — filter by record label (e.g. Blue Note, Trojan)
  • Year — narrow to a specific release year
  • Genre & Style — filter by broad genre, then drill into sub-styles
  • Format — Vinyl, CD, Cassette, and more

Result Types

Use the radio buttons to filter what kind of results appear:

  • All — mix of releases, artists, labels, and masters
  • Masters — canonical versions of recordings (groups all pressings)
  • Releases — specific pressings (country, year, format)
  • Artists — artist pages with discography
  • Labels — record label pages with full catalogue

✦ AI Search

Select AI to ask Claude for music recommendations in plain language — "obscure 70s Nigerian funk", "music like early Miles Davis", "best post-war Chicago blues guitarists". Claude returns 6–8 curated picks, each linking directly to a Discogs search. Requires a free account.

Sort

Sort results by relevance, year (newest or oldest first), title, or artist. Sort applies when you click Search.

Result Cards

Click any result card to see full release details — tracklist, credits, pressing info, market price, and photos. Click the cover photo to browse all available images. Artist and label names link to their full Discogs pages within SeaDisco.

Recent Searches Feed

The strip at the bottom shows recent searches from all SeaDisco users — click any pill to run that search instantly.

Sharing a Search

Every search lives in the URL — just copy it from your browser and send it to anyone. They'll land on exactly the same results, no account needed.

My Collection & Wantlist

Signed-in users with a Discogs token can browse their Discogs collection and wantlist directly from the main page using the tabs above results. Records are synced from Discogs (up to 1,000 at a time) and cached locally for fast browsing. Green ✓ Collection and red ♡ Wantlist badges appear on search results. Click 'Sync now' to refresh.

Your Account

Create a free account and connect your own Discogs API token for unlimited searches and a personal search history. Get your token at discogs.com → Settings → Developers → Generate new token.
