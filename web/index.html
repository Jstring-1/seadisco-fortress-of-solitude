How SeaDisco Works

Searching

Search the entire Discogs database by artist, album, label, or any combination. Click Advanced for targeted fields (Artist, Release, Label, Year, Genre/Style, Format). Narrow by type — Masters (every pressing grouped) or Releases (individual pressings). Sort by relevance, year, title, or label.

Click any card for full details: tracklist with YouTube previews, version finder, pressing info, credits, market price, and photos. Hide owned removes synced collection items from results. Select ✦ AI to describe what you want in plain language — Claude returns 12 curated picks with Discogs links.

Latest Drops

150 random picks from a rolling 3-month database of 20,000+ releases via ListenBrainz/MusicBrainz, refreshed every 6 hours. Filter by the tag cloud or genre dropdown, or search the full database by artist, title, or tag.

Click any card to search Discogs — if matched, the full popup opens with alternatives. No match shows ListenBrainz info with clickable tags and a Google link.

Live

Search upcoming concerts and events by artist, city/zip code, or genre. Results pull from both Ticketmaster and Bandsintown, merged and deduped. Artist searches include a Bandsintown photo when available. Each listing links to Google for tickets. Your last searched city is saved for convenience.

Gear

Browse vintage hi-fi and audio equipment listings from eBay, updated daily. Filter by price tier — $100+, $200+, $500+, or $1,000+ — and scroll through receivers, amplifiers, turntables, speakers, and more. Each card links directly to the eBay listing.

Collection, Wantlist & Wants

Add your Discogs token to sync your full collection and wantlist — any size. Sync runs in the background and refreshes automatically every 6 hours. Both tabs have dedicated filters (artist, release, label, year, genre, style, format) with + (AND), | (OR), and - (exclude) operators. Green ✓ and red ♡ badges mark owned and wanted items across all results.

The Wants tab shows 500 random wantlist items pooled from all SeaDisco members — a quick way to see what collectors are after.

Account & Limits

Anonymous users are capped at 5 searches/day on a shared token. Sign up free and add your own Discogs token (discogs.com → Settings → Developers) for unlimited searches. The recent search cloud below results shows interesting searches from all users — click any pill to re-run it.
