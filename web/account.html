SeaDisco is powered by the Discogs music database. Free visitors get a handful of searches — connect your own free Discogs token and you get your own unlimited search quota, collection and wantlist syncing, and a curated music news feed.

Here's all it takes:

  1. Create a free account at discogs.com (if you don't have one)
  2. Go to Settings → Developers and click Generate new token
  3. Come back here, paste it in, and you're done

No password needed — signing up just requires an email and a one-time verification code. Your Discogs token is stored securely and only used to search on your behalf. We never see your Discogs password.

Your Discogs Token

A Discogs Personal Access Token lets SeaDisco search Discogs on your behalf using your own rate limits. Without one, searches are limited to 5 per day.

How to get your token:
1. Go to discogs.com/settings/developers
2. Click Generate new token
3. Copy and paste it below
