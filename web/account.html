SeaDisco is powered by the Discogs music database. Free visitors get a handful of searches — sign in and connect your free Discogs account in one click to unlock your own unlimited search quota, collection and wantlist syncing, ratings, folder management, and a curated music news feed.

Here's all it takes:

  1. Enter your email below (no password — just a one-time verification code; new or returning, same box)
  2. Click Connect with Discogs and approve access on Discogs
  3. That's it — your collection is yours to browse, rate, and organize

SeaDisco never sees your Discogs password. The connection is a standard Discogs OAuth authorization, and you can disconnect anytime. Prefer a Personal Access Token? That option is still available after sign-in.

Registration is limited. SeaDisco has a small number of spots available.

Accounts inactive for 6 months are automatically hibernated to free up spots for others. Hibernated accounts can reactivate when a spot opens up — no data is deleted.

Connect with Discogs

One click to connect your Discogs account. OAuth gives full access including marketplace listing management (create, edit, delete), seller order management, and messaging with buyers — features not available with a Personal Access Token.

Your Discogs Token

The easiest way to connect is the Connect with Discogs button above — no token needed. A Personal Access Token is an alternative if you prefer.

A Discogs Personal Access Token lets SeaDisco search Discogs on your behalf using your own rate limits. Without one, searches are limited to 5 per day.

How to get your token:
1. Go to discogs.com/settings/developers
2. Click Generate new token
3. Copy and paste it below
