SeaDisco is powered by the Discogs music database. Free visitors get a handful of searches — connect your own free Discogs token and you get your own unlimited search quota and your last 50 searches saved to your account.

Here's all it takes:

  1. Create a free account at discogs.com (if you don't have one)
  2. Go to Settings → Developers and click Generate new token
  3. Come back here, paste it in, and you're done

Your token is stored securely and only used to make Discogs searches on your behalf. We never see your Discogs password.

Your Discogs Token

A Discogs Personal Access Token lets SeaDisco search Discogs on your behalf using your own rate limits — and saves your 50 most recent searches to your account. Without one, searches are limited to 5 per day.

How to get your token:
1. Go to discogs.com/settings/developers
2. Click Generate new token
3. Copy and paste it below
